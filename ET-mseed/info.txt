All of the files correspond to Einstein Telescope Tests, which are available at https://eida.ingv.it/en/getdata/3M_2024 and are from 01-11-2024 to 02-11-2025 (DD-MM-YYYY)
It is important to point out that the first leters "FAB??" correspond to the respective station code of the network 3M_2024, available at https://www.orfeus-eu.org/stationbook/networks/3M/2024/
All of them are "HH?" type, which means "High Broadband" or "High-Gain Broadband"
The tird letter "Z/N/E" stands for the orientation Vertical/North-South/East-Weast
More information about the naming convention is available at https://epic.earthscope.org/webfm_send/2134
You can also check https://docs.obspy.org/tutorial/index.html for a quick tutorial on how Obspy works
